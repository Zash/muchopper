Domain listing criteria

There are no criteria for a domain to be included in the crawl, except that we need to know about it.

Domain delisting

If you are the owner of a domain and want it to be completely excluded from the listing, please get in touch. Be prepared to provide proof that you are the owner of the domain. A good (zero-roundtrip) way to do so is to contact us from an XMPP address which is listed in your services contact addresses.

Traffic

We crawl all known domains for public rooms approximately once per hour. In addition, we query all rooms we know for their meta-information in the same rhythm. We rate-limit our requests and shuffle the list of rooms to spread the load evenly across the time window.

If you still find that we cause too much traffic on your domain, please get in touch (see above). We can work out to reduce the frequency for your domain or apply other means.
